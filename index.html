About Me:

Hi there, My name is Jonathan Shwartz, 20 years old, currently living in Rishon Lezion, Israel. Between ages 5-17, I played professional basketball- my favorite sport. Unfortunately, I had to stop, due to an injury, and was looking for something I love doing, in order to fill the void basketball had left in my life.

I chose to work in another field, which combined my love for food and people, and started working at "Mexicana" restaurant in Rishon Lezion. A few months later, I was promoted to the position of branch manager, and worked there until I was drafted to the army.

My main hobbies are basketball, fitness, cooking and hiking. In addition, I have always enjoyed disassembling, assembling and repairing technological devices (such as iOS, Android and Windows) and I like to help people who encounter such problems. My passion for computers and troubleshooting has led me to the decision to study QA and develop in this field.
